The Team

Leadership Roles

Connie Jiang

Team Captain

Connie is a senior double majoring in Chemical and Material Science Engineering. Connie loves how the team combines her affinity for water and canoes with the creative engineering required to make concrete float. She's been active since sophomore year, serving as the treasurer last year. Outside of concrete canoe, Connie's also involved with Habitat for Humanity. She likes to sing karaoke and break out into dance randomly in public. As Captain, Connie has high hopes for this year and the team.

Tommy Tang

Concrete Design

Tommy is a senior studying Industrial and Operational Engineering from Brooklyn, New York. Tommy joined the team his freshman year and is excited to lead the concrete design for this year’s canoe. In his free time he enjoys basketball, ultimate frisbee, and other sports.

Olivia Mitchinson

Hull Design

Olivia is a sophomore this year returning for her second year on the team. She is originally from Grand Blanc, Michigan. She loves being on a small team full of very friendly people. In her free time she likes to listen to music, collect plants, and watch Netflix.

Mackenzie Horton

Public Relations

Mackenzie is a sophomore studying Chemical Engineering from Howell, MI. This is her second year on the team. Her favorite part of being on the team, besides her teammates, is other people’s interpretation of what the Concrete Canoe team does. In her spare time she enjoys listening to music, dancing, and spending time adventuring with friends.

Laura Zeiler

Secretary

Laura is a junior from Beverly Hills, MI. She is studying Industrial and Operations Engineering and is also a member of the Program in Sustainable Engineering. This is her second year on the team and first year as secretary. Laura is also a member of Central Student Government and the Tech Day Planning Committee.

Mehul Kulkarni

Treasurer

Mehul hails from the distant town of Saline, MI. He is a sophomore majoring in Civil Engineering. He enjoys playing the violin, dancing, and watching excessive amounts of the Food Network. He is excited for his second year on the Concrete Canoe Team and is excited to continue being a part of a close knit group of hard working students.

Lauren Eastes

Construction/Aesthetics Lead

Lauren is a senior originally from Troy, MI and is studying Civil and Environmental Engineering at the university, because two majors is double the fun. Adding Concrete Canoe is basically infinity fun. She joined the team her sophomore year because it sounded super awesome and the other members of the team gave it a really comfortable and relaxed atmosphere. In her spare time, she enjoys listening to music and trying to find obscure bands so she can be pretentious about listening to them.

Christopher Giddley

Quality Control

Gabe is a junior studying Biomedical Engineering on a pre-med track because why not take the hardest path to medical school. Continuing that theme further, he joined Concrete Canoe because making a canoe out of concrete makes perfect sense. This is his third year on the team as he loves everyone on the team and enjoys making a brand new canoe year after year. He is from Houston, Texas, a huge soccer fan, and on ResStaff in West Quad.

Eric O'Neill

Paddle Practice Lead

In his spare time Eric plays IM sports, studies a lot, and hangs out with his friends. Eric was on the team last year and is looking forward to a great year with great people.
